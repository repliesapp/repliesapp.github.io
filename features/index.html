Replies Features

Reuse Answers

Replies turns your replies into a knowledge base.

A message will be separated into chunks of reusable snippets. They do also remember attachments and help you sending meaningful answers within seconds.

Prioritize

Replies smartly prioritizes incoming requests.

It helps you focus on most important request first without having to constantly browse through your inbox.

Keep Track

Never forget a follow-up.

Messages can be marked as pending or resolved. That makes it easy to keep track of messages where you need to follow up later-on.

Know your Customer

Quickly browse through recent conversations.

Sometimes it's hard to remember who's on the other end. Replies helps you to personalize your message by showing you recent interactions with that person.

Undo Support

Never send a message by accident anymore.

Replies doesn't send a message immediately. It gives you about 15 seconds to undo a send action and thus prevents you from unintentional transmissions.
