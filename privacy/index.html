Privacy Policy

Effective February 4, 2011

Uncomplex respects your privacy. Therefore, when you do business with us, we will not:

  • Sell or distribute your personal information unless it is required for purchasing a product or service.
  • Email you without your request to do so.
  • Deny your right to be removed from our mailing list. We will remove your name immediately.
  • Retain full details of your credit card information. The only exclusion regards services requiring recurring billing.

In order to provide you with future service, Uncomplex maintains information in a specific order, such as resending lost serial numbers. Please note that we take extreme precautions concerning the safety of all personal information.
